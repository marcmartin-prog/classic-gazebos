The Quality Difference

Materials

The only material that makes it thru our rigorous selection process is #1 or better dried after treated lumber. It is planed and routered to ensure a smooth finish and rounded edges. The roof decking for shingles is 3/4 inch plywood for a durable roof with no sags.

Options

You have control over the final outcome. You choose your favorite color of architectural shingles, or metal roofing. Add options like screens or whatever you want to make it match your property.

Building

Expert builders with years of experience spend days crafting your beautiful new gazebo, using ultra weather resistant fasteners, making sure everything is put together properly.
