Picnic Tables

Make family memories in the great outdoors around a poly picnic table. Super easy to clean up after a meal, and will never lose it's beautiful colors. They are designed to prevent sagging from cold and heat. All are built with a welded aluminum frame for maximum strength and durability.

Octagon Picnic Tables

Durable octagon picnic tables look great on your patio. Constructed from our sustainable Tangent poly lumber, they will last a lifetime. Easily disassemble with no fasteners for storage or transport. Umbrella holes in the center are standard, so you can sit in the shade.

Adirondack Chairs

These comfortable poly adirondack chairs are the perfect addition to your happy place. Choose between regular adirondack chairs, or a folding version if you ever need to store or move them. Make the children happy with a children's size adirondack chair. Prop up your feet with the ottoman made to match.

Glider Chairs

Glider and swivel glider chairs take relaxing on your deck to a new level of comfort. Swivels are made of stainless steel for a long-lasting smooth swivel. For the longest life possible, we also only use stainless steel ball bearing brackets on your poly gliders.

Glider Benches

Enjoy nature with the people you love with a poly glider or bench. Available in 4' and 5' lengths, in either a bench or glider style. With an optional folding center console, you can choose between more seating or refreshing drinks. Also, choose between fanback and colonial styles to match your other decor. No matter which you choose, you can trust that it will be the ultimate in durability and comfort.

Gliding Pub Chairs and Teta-A-Tete

Gliding pub chairs are a higher version of our chair gliders, with all the same stainless steel hardware and quality build. Choose between 24" and 30" seat height. You can buy a set of two with a detachable table and cupholder between them.
