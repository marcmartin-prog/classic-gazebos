Materials

The only material that makes it thru our rigorous selection process is #1 or better dried after treated lumber. All fasteners are specially coated corrosion resistant screws.

Building

All lumber is planed before assembly and the corners are routered for smoothness and carefully fitted together to give you a beautiful product.
