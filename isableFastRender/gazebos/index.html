The Quality Difference

Materials

The only material that makes it thru our rigorous selection process is #1 or better dried after treated lumber. It is planed and routered to ensure a smooth finish and rounded edges. The roof decking for shingles is 3/4 inch plywood for a durable roof with no sags.

Options

You have control over the final outcome. You choose your favorite color of architectural shingles, or metal roofing. Add options like screens or whatever you want to make your new gazebo match your property.

Building

Expert builders with years of experience spend days crafting your beautiful new gazebo, using ultra weather resistant fasteners, making sure everything is put together properly.

Options
For Your Gazebo

Screening

Get your gazebo screened in to allow you to enjoy it without insects bothering you. A screened-in gazebo is the best place to relax on a buggy southern evening. Make a party and don't invite the insects. We build our gazebos in Northern Arkansas, so we know how annoying bugs are in the south.

Colors

Choose your roof colors to get a look that fits well wherever you place your custom gazebo. There's a wide range of colors for your classic gazebo, which makes it easy to get exactly the look that fits in with your landscaping or house.

Roof

You can choose between architectural shingles or a metal roof. Both are extremely durable options, keeping your gazebo dry for years to come. Never worry about Arkansas storms with a well-built roof.

Benches

Increase ease and comfort by adding built-in benches to your classic gazebo. Host parties or family time in your gazebo without dragging chairs around. The benches run around the inside of the gazebo. Catch the beauty of an Arkansas, Texas, Missouri, or Kansas view, right from the comfort of your gazebo benches.

Sealing

Add durability and beauty by adding a quality sealer to your wooden gazebo to keep the wood from deteriorating over time. Looks great, and stays looking great, with minimal maintenance, even through the heat of summer and the wind and rain of winter.
