About Us

Floyd Yoder Started Classic Gazebos in 1995 to fill a local need for quality gazebos and lawn furniture. Over the years, we’ve focused on quality craftmanship and customer service to give you the best product and buying experience.

In 2017 Terry Hostetler bought the business and continued the legacy of quality work and great customer service. We are a family owned and operated company, working together to provide great craftsmanship. Three of Terry’s sons work in the facility as well and help with delivery. From our family to yours, our goal is to create beautiful lawn furniture and gazebos that make great family memories for you!
