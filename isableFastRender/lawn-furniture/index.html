Where Quality Comes Built In

Materials

The only material that makes it thru our rigorous selection process is #1 or better dried after treated lumber. All fasteners are specially coated corrosion resistant screws.

Sealing

Our lawn furniture is treated with a high quality cedar tone water sealer on all pieces, unless special ordered otherwise.

Building

All lumber is planed before assembly and the corners are routered for smoothness and carefully fitted together to give you a beautiful product.

What We Build

Picnic Tables

Make family memories in the great outdoors around a handcrafted cedar stained picnic table. These wooden tables will look great on a patio or backyard.

Lawn Bridges

Easily remove obstacles like a stream or a ditch with our lawn bridges. These strong wooden bridges keep you on solid ground even in the muddy spring.

Gliders

Choose from several styles of gliders for your patio or lawn. Regular gliders have full length seating, or a snack glider allows you to satisfy those cravings with a built in table.

Swings and Benches

Swings that you can place anywhere, or an arbor with a swing already hung, ready to place anywhere. These Arkansas-made swings are great for children or just to watch a sunset.

Arbors

Beautiful arbors for your path or garden. Make the plants happy with a Classic Gazebos arbor for them to climb. Adds a touch like no other to your pathway, yard, or garden.
